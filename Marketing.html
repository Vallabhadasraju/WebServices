Description

Marketing is the strategic process of promoting, selling, and distributing a product or service to target consumers. It involves market research, branding, advertising, and customer engagement to drive demand and sales. Effective marketing combines psychology, data analysis, and creativity to influence consumer behavior. Businesses use various channels, including digital, social media, content marketing, and traditional advertising, to reach their audience. The goal is to create value, build relationships, and differentiate from competitors.
